Our Services

We provide on-demand online medical consultations for mild cases that do not require a physical examination in which otherwise the patients may resort to self medication because of lack of access to a qualified medical professional. We provide these services through the following:

1. Video call through our web, iOS and Android platforms.

2. Artificial Intelligence powered symptoms checker.

3. Hardware that allows patients to talk to a doctor in remote resource constrained settings.

Conditions for Using Our Platform
